Extra information to help your studying

Time Management Skills

  1. A good early to the day is vital. If you get up late you may not have time to eat and as a result not have the energy to do you best or you could forget something important like your pencil case or homework which would throw off your entire day.
  2. Going to bed early will make it easier to get up early. Sleep is invaluable. User your time profitably and turn off the video games to get a good night's sleep.
  3. Be realistic, prioritize based on how long you have to complete each task. Highest priority is something due today or tomorrow, second priority due in one week, third priority due in one month and perhaps spending longer on the high priority tasks and get them finished first.
  4. Extracurricular activites are invaluable, you can learn skills, get active and make friends. But how many is too many, how do you balance extracurricular with school work? As you get older you have to learn to set priorities that will benefit you in the long run.
  5. Even leisure time is valuable so don't waste it on time consuming social media/computer games/TV etc. that will give you nothing back. Learn an instrument, learn a language, meet new people, be proactive and engage with the world.
